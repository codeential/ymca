OUR MISSION

About Our Cause

YMCA Nizamuddin, a unit of New Delhi YMCA, has been serving the underprivileged communities of Nizamuddin Basti since 1927. We provide comprehensive child welfare programs focusing on education, nutrition, and holistic development.

Education Support

Providing quality education, school supplies, tutoring, and scholarship programs to help children excel academically.

Nutrition Program

Daily nutritious meals, health supplements, and regular health check-ups ensuring children grow healthy and strong.
